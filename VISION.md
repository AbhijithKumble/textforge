## Why Another Regex Project?

Regex tutorials already exist.

Many of them are excellent.

The problem is that they primarily teach the syntax of regular expressions.

Professional developers rarely sit down thinking:

"I need to practice quantifiers."

Instead, they think:

"I need to extract every IP address from this log."

TextForge starts from real-world problems rather than language features.

Regex is only the first step.

The long-term goal is to teach the complete Unix text-processing ecosystem.
